Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first resume?

Creating your first resume is easy with ResumeBuilder:

  1. Click on "Get Started" or "Build Your CV" button
  2. Choose a template that fits your style and industry
  3. Follow the guided process to add your personal information, work experience, education, and skills
  4. Customize colors and formatting as needed
  5. Download your completed resume in PDF format or share it online
Are your templates ATS-friendly?

Yes, all our templates are designed to be ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly. They use standard sections and formatting that can be easily read by automated systems, while still looking professional and visually appealing to human recruiters.

Is ResumeBuilder free to use?

We offer both free and premium options:

  • Free Plan: Access to basic templates, resume creation tools, and PDF downloads
  • Premium Plan: Access to all templates, advanced customization options, cover letter tools, multiple download formats, and removal of watermarks
Can I create multiple resumes?

Yes! You can create multiple versions of your resume for different job applications. This allows you to tailor your resume for specific roles or industries, which increases your chances of getting noticed by employers.

How do I update my resume after I've created it?

Updating your resume is simple. Just log into your account, navigate to "My Resumes," select the resume you want to edit, and make your changes. All your resumes are saved in your account for easy access and updates.

What file formats can I download my resume in?

You can download your resume in the following formats:

  • PDF (recommended for most job applications)
  • DOCX (Microsoft Word)
  • TXT (plain text format)
  • HTML (for online sharing)

Premium users also have access to additional formats.
